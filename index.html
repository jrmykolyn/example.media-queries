Width

Width-based media queries are commonly used to determine when to switch between layouts (eg. from a single to a multi-column design).

(min-width: 0px)
(max-width: 1200px)
(min-width: 600px) and ( max-width: 768px )

Height

Height-based media queries are also very useful, both on their own and when combined with min-width or max-width queries.

(min-height: 600px)
(max-height: 1000px)
(min-height: 500px) and ( max-height: 750 )

Aspect Ratio

The aspect ratio media queries can be used to apply styles when: the viewport is an exact ratio (eg. 1/1 or square); the viewport is equal to or greater than a specific ratio; or the viewport is equal to or less than a specific ratio.

(aspect-ratio: 1/1)
(min-aspect-ratio: 3/2)
(max-aspect-ratio: 3/4)

Orientation

This media query is used to apply styles based on whether the viewport is in a portrait or landscape orientation.

(orientation: landscape)
(orientation: portrait)
